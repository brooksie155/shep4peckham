Independent  for  Peckham Rye

I'm Claire Sheppard, a proud Peckham local for the past 17 years.

I live with my husband, our little boy and our chocolate Lab. My husband and I both run small businesses in SE15 so the future of this area is close to our hearts. I'm not a member of a political party; I'm standing as an Independent candidate here in Peckham Rye Ward in the Southwark council elections on May 3rd 2018.
